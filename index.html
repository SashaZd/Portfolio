SASHA AZAD


Hi! I just graduated from the MS Computer Science degree program at Georgia Tech specializing in Interactive Intelligence. My research has been primarily focussed in the areas of Entertainment Intelligence, Narrative Intelligence and Mixed Reality Interaction.

For my Masters Research Project I developed an agumented reality game for the Hololens that combines Procedural Content Generation, Puzzle Generation and Artificial Intelligence. Last summer I was the Enterpreneurial Lead in an NSF iCorps Grant that researched development of a knowledge extraction tool for financial institutions.

You can generally find me at the Entertainment Intelligence Lab, the Design Intelligence Lab or the AppLab@RNOC, at Georgia Tech working on some really cool project.

Highlighted Projects
Exploring Mixed Reality, PCG & Game AI

R&D of a mixed reality interactive experience that responds to changes in the user's environment. The game is aimed at exploring how Procedural Content Generation and Game AI can augment the user's experience on the Microsoft Hololens. This project was my Masters Research Project.

Characterizing the Marvel Comic Universe

Designed a interactive visualization for Marvel comic book enthusiasts and aggregate media analysts to explore the vast variety of Marvel characters, their relationships to one another and to their universe. Also developed a relationtional database and API to support the interaction.
Knowledge Based AI

Developed a Python agent that could solve the 2x1, 2x2, 3x3 IQ test using KBAI techniques such as Case Based Reasoning, Learning by Recording Cases, Problem Reduction, Generate and Test, etc.
Multiplayer RPG Murder Mystery Game

Designed an AI quest generator and simulation based story generator to create an RPG for users to play on their iOS devices. Players solve quests for clues, and question other players to discover the murderer.
