Highlighted Projects
Exploring Augmented Reality, PCG & Game AI

Design and development of an AR interactive experience that responds to changes in the user's environment. The game is aimed at exploring how players using the Microsoft Hololens manipulate their environment to produce new game experiences. This project is currently my Masters Research Project.

Characterizing the Marvel Comic Universe

Designed a interactive visualization for Marvel comic book enthusiasts and fans to explore the vast variety of Marvel characters, their relationships to one another and to their universe. Also developed a relationtional database and API to support the interaction.

Designed an AI quest generator and simulation based story generator to create an RPG for users to play on their iOS devices. Players solve quests for clues, and question other players to discover the murderer.
